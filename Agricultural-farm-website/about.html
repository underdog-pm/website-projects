About Us

Dear visitor,
Our Mission: At Wakulima Hodari Farm, our mission is to promote sustainable agriculture practices and provide high-quality produce to our customers. We are committed to growing our crops in a way that is environmentally responsible and socially beneficial, while also ensuring the health and well-being of our community.
Values:Community: We believe in being an active member of our community, and we are committed to supporting local initiatives and organizations that promote sustainable agriculture, education, and community development.
We are a vibrant and progressive agricultural farm located in Eldoret, Kenya. Established in 1995 by a group of four students from University of Eldoret, our farm has grown to become a leading producer of high-quality agricultural products in the region. We specialize in a variety of crops including maize, beans, peas, and vegetables such as cabbages, carrots, and onions. Our farm is equipped with modern farming technologies and equipment, which help us to produce high yields of quality produce. We also employ sustainable farming practices, which ensure that our farm remains productive for generations to come. At Mkulima Hodari, we take pride in the quality of our produce. We have a team of skilled and experienced farm workers who are dedicated to ensuring that our produce meets the highest standards of quality. We also have a quality control team, which ensures that all our produce is thoroughly inspected before it is released into the market. Our farm also serves as a training center for aspiring farmers. We offer training on sustainable farming practices, modern farming technologies, and crop management. We believe that by sharing our knowledge and expertise with others, we can help to promote sustainable agriculture in our region. We are committed to providing our customers with fresh and healthy produce. Our farm is conveniently located near major markets, which allows us to deliver our products quickly and efficiently.Whether you are a local resident looking for fresh, locally grown produce or a wholesale buyer interested in our bulk offerings we invite you contact us. We offer affordable prices, which makes our products accessible to a wide range of consumers.

Get to know us

28 Years Agriculture Experience

Wakulima Hodari Farm is a leading agricultural farm located in Eldoret, Kenya, with a history of producing high-quality crops and promoting sustainable agriculture practices. Established in 1995 by a group of four students from University of Eldoret, our farm is committed to providing our customers with fresh, healthy, and delicious produce grown using environmentally responsible farming practices. Our team of passionate farmers are dedicated to sharing our knowledge and expertise with others through training and education initiatives. We are also actively involved in supporting local community development initiatives. At Wakulima Hodari Farm, we are committed to promoting sustainable agriculture and improving the livelihoods of farmers in our region.
