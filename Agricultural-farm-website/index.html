What We’re Offering

Agriculture Products

Wakulima farm and Farmers offer a range of services for crops, including crop production, harvesting, and transportation.









Organic Products

We offer a wide range of organic products, including fruits, vegetables, grains, and dairy products. All of our organic products are grown and produced using sustainable and environmentally-friendly methods.





Fresh Vegetables

Wakulima Hodari farm offer a wide range of fresh vegetable products, including leafy greens such as Nightshade(Managu), root vegetables such onions, and seasonal produce. Our vegetables are harvested at the peak of freshness and are available for purchase directly from the farm or through local retailers.

Dairy Products

We offer a variety of dairy products, including milk, cheese, and yogurt. Our dairy products are made from the milk of our grass-fed cows and are free from artificial hormones and antibiotics.
